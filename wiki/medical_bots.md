Robots already have, and will increasingly have an important part to play in the treatment of medical illnesses.

Most likely this will eventually be the realm of [Nano Bots](/wiki/nano_bots.html "Microscopic or") but until then robot enhanced surgery is now very much a trusted, tried and tested application.
