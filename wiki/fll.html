The First Lego League is a competition which is part of the First Robotics Group. It is aimed at using Lego Mindstorms kits like the RCX and later the NXT to teach kids science, technology and engineering skills.

They hold regular challenges, and have similar goals and aspirations to the OrionRobots clubs and events which are held from time to time.
