These devices are commonly used to extend the output abilities of microcontrollers so they can link with more Servos. Using a Serial Data Stream with a simple 2 wire protocol - you can usally control 6 additional Servos.

The SSC is normally based upon a small, mass produced MicroController which simply takes those signals, and stores the instructions - maintaining the Servos at the desired position.

They normally have a very small footprint - mostly dictated by the connectors. Most good robot component stores have at least one range.
