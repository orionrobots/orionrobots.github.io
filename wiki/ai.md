</p>

<br/>There are many techniques which fall under this banner
</p>
<p>Artificial Life

<a href="/wiki/genetic_algorithm.html" title="Genetic Algorithm">Genetic Algorithms</a>
<br/>Neural Networks

<br/>Natural Language Processing

<br/>Subsumption Architecture

<br/>Expert Systems

</p>
