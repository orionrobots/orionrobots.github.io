<p>BrickShelf accompanies <a class="wiki" href="/wiki/lugnet.html" title="Lego Users Group Network">Lugnet</a> as the primary place on the internet for showing off <a class="wiki" href="/wiki/lego.html" title="The best known construction toy">Lego</a> creations, also known as MOC's. Every user gets their own gallery space, and can put models up, subject to review and moderation.
</p>
<p>It has a massive collection of images, as well as accompanying text and also <a class="wiki" href="/wiki/ldraw_system.html" title="The primary system for CAD representation of Lego parts">LDraw System</a> <a class="wiki" href="/wiki/cad.html" title="Computer Aided Design">CAD</a> files for some models which you could then build yourself.
</p>
<p>Any <a class="wiki" href="/wiki/lego.html" title="The best known construction toy">Lego</a> creator would be well advised to take a look there for inspiration and to see what other people are building.
</p>
