<p>A cyborg is defined as a creation (machine or animal) that was part organic and part mechanical.
</p>
<p>Hollywood have loved the idea of monster combinations of man and machine - but the reality may be a great deal more subtle than that. The 64 billion dollar man, the bionic woman, The Terminator<a class="wiki wikinew for-review" title="Create page: The Terminator">?</a> are a few examples of Cybernetic Organisms.
</p>
<p>A person having Cybernetic Implants<a class="wiki wikinew for-review" title="Create page: Cybernetic Implants">?</a> could technically be considered a cyborg. This at the present includes anyone with a heart regulator, or artificial limb or similar. There is these days a great deal of the body which may be substituted for a mechanical replacement for medical reasons - and this list is expanding.
</p>
