This is a community adopted term for those who have found that in there adulthood, they are still interesting in building with Lego. With the Lego Mindstorms project, this community has grown massively, and the new NXT will increase this furthar.

Many of the AFOLs are to be found posting, or at least lurking on Lugnet Robotics.
