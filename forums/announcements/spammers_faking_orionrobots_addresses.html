f you receive any email from orionrobots.co.uk that has an attachment please delete it without opening. We have found that spammers are faking our email addresses to spam people.

Our own servers are protected, and to some degree block spam - but we cannot prevent other people faking our credentials.

I recommend other forum sites use some form of spam database poisoning like our own, as it is inevitably sites like Lugnet where the addresses have been harvested.

OrionRobots itself protects addresses with spam poison - which in effect fills a spammers database with random email addresses (things like sgsdfghslgksahgsag at tegsaxfbvxmn.com) in an attempt to ruin their harvested list.

Orion
