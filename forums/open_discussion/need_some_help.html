Hi There Vincent,

This is one of those "How long is a piece of string" kind of questions. How much do you want to spend? What functionality do you want, how many movement areas (Degrees of Freedom? How many and what kind of sensors will you need?

How much computational power (onboard or outboard) would you want to throw at it? How much time to research a system like horse and rider, BEAM or Subsumption architecture? What tools do you have - for example a PCB etch lab and plastic vacuum former?

When you say "life size", I presume you mean a human like android. These can be pretty expensive, as you will require many high-torque servo''s, probably ones with feedback to the actual CPU. For projects like that, look up Open PINO? and COG. Pino has full plans for building a human like robot platform for experimentation.

I would probably put aside a basic budget of between 1 and 5 thousand pounds to start it off.

Regards,
Danny
