It may be possible to create a Serial link between the Lego RCX and the Lego CyberMaster using a sensor port on the RCX and one sensors port and one motor port on the CyberMaster. It may also be possible between two RCX''s.

The RCX sensor port is capable of sampling(sensing) and in active mode can produce a signal(go in and out of active mode - creating mode changes). Now I am not sure that the Cybermaster can do the same - it may mean you have to use a Motor port for output, and a sensor port for input.

Why would you do this? Firstly - if you have a CyberMaster and RCX, you can free up the limitations of IR, and use a wireless communication system with the CyberMaster to control the bots(although you will not be able to upload programs to the RCX in this way). Secondly - it means you can get the two systems to work together for a bot with more sensors, and more motors, as well as double the processing ability. Using IR communications between two RCX''s is fairly limited - and also requires line-of-sight.

The CyberMasters motor port will be outputting PWM - which is a fairly course signal, and makes it not suitable for serial output . A PWM device even on full power does not output a straight line signal.

Does anyone have any ideas or experience with this?
