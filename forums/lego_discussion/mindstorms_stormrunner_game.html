To get to the energy sensor:

Applied method: distraction

Build a Hermes and an Achilles-class, equip the Hermes(I did it with the Achilles, but the Hermes is faster..., both work) with a grabber-arm. Nothing more.

Get both in the Tormod. Then position the Achilles so that it will drive from the right to the left underneath the eater so it gets eaten. "BUT WAIT" first you must position the Hermes in the top left corner facing down so all you have to do is make it move forward one square, turn right, pick the thing up(don''t store), turn right again, move into the safe zone.

First you activate the Achilles, the moment the plant moves to eat it you activate the Hermes. Now you should have The Energy sensor.

Here are its stats:

P:3
E:2
L:2
weight:0.05

Have fun.
