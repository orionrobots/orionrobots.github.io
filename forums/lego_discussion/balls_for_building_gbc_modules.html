Well according to one fellow on Lugnet - a Pick A Brick (PaB) store in Oberhausen, Germany has the balls at 0.50 Euros each. This is not really great if you are looking to buy a whole cart-load though.

I would still recommend bricklink. Maybe if enough people get together they could email Lego and get them to sell bulk ball packs. Remember that the Basket Balls are the same dimensions as the footballs, and having a good mix of them means that you will have a more interesting looking machine.

This also leaves the potential of being able to use a light-sensor to try and separate them.
