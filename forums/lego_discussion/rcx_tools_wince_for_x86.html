AFAIK - there is an NQC port which runs under WinCE - although it may be interesting to see how you will transfer the programs.

Some, but not all PDA and WinCE boxes have IR ports which can encode signals the RCX can use, although the tool you have in mind only supports an interesting arrangement of Null-Modem cables connected to the tower.

Anyway - the binaries, and contact information for that is here:
http://www.rainer-keuchel.de/rcx/rcx.html

There appear to be what might by wince-x86 binaries, but do contact the page owners there if you cannot find the exact build you want.
