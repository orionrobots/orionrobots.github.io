Its fairly normal that boards like these - with no soldering are used to build digitally controlled robots - there are very few builders who would roll their own pcb for that sort of thing.

My thoughts on this are that maybe something like the OOPic R or Brainstem would be better. In the states you would probably want to shop at http://robotstore.com or http://acroname.com.

Do you consider the RC thing essential?
