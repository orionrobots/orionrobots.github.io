My advice would be to look at the Fischertechnik kits.

Lego have a pneumatic system, but they do not include solenoid valves. They have double acting cylinders (large and small), and older sets had single acting cylinders and two way non-return valves (on Peeron as 3 way manifold). They have simple 2 way valves - with an obscured exhaust port.

Schools often used to use Fischertechnik to teach pneumatic systems - as they have double and single acting cylinders, solenoid valves, and also come with handy compressors - which you do not get with Lego. They do still sell sets, and also have a robot interface of their own to experiment with.

Outside of these two systems - there are parts catalogue and such, but if you are experimenting, or building small scale - then you will probably find other offerings fairly dissapointing.

Orion
