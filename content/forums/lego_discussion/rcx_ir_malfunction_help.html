Okay - do you know anybody who has the Serial Based Tower? It is worth a try, although from what you say - it sounds like the RCX IR receiver may be at fault. The thing is, it depends on weather the tower must do a successful handshake with the RCX to start it responding.

I cant say I recall the exact details of the communication protocol, but I would suspect no IR activity from the RCX until it has successfully picked something up - it would not make any sense to fire signals from the IR LED on the device until it has a reason to.

It is worth contacting your local Lego group representatives. Lego are normally fairly helpful with these things, and may be able to service it for you. I doubt any else can, although if you post to Lugnet Robotics, then somebody there may have enough experience to service it - there are people on it who have produced partial RCX schematics.

Orion
