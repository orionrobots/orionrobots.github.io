I think by far the best uk group for robotics discussion is www.robotbuilder.co.uk certainly the one I use most and what seems a good spread of technical contributors.

The picaxe forum is great for topics relating to the low cost controller itself and some general solutions arising from use of controllers.

www.electro-tech-online.com (UK based) has a robotics chat forum and some interesting posts from time to time

comp.robotics.misc is good but answers tend to have a USA based answer (especially when you''re looking for components) and uk.tech.robotics just tends to be _very_ quiet

orionrobots.co.uk is intended to cover a broad audience - including
complete beginners. Depending on where you are based, you may be able to
attend our Saturday classes - which are mindstorms based for kids in the
morning, and less so for the adults in the afternoons. We are based in
London.

Another suggestion is the DPRG list, and also comp.robotics.misc might be
worth checking out.

Anyway - if you have a question that is non mindstorms, feel free to ask
it at the forums on Orionrobots.
