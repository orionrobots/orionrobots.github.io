After last weeks Coder Dojo and Kingston University, some of the robots in our robot fleet needed repair.

These Raspberry Pi RP2040 (Pico) based robots are designed for learning and run micropython. Each has motors, a pair of distance sensors, a line sensor, a bank of blue display LEDs plus two RGB LEDs. They have room for expansion with encoders or servo motors.

The video below shows my step by step teardown of one of the robots, to investigate and repair the issues.
