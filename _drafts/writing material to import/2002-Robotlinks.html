Open Pino

Open Pino is a fantastic idea. It is an open source robotics platform. Albeit a little expensive to build it is fairly comprehensive.
And best of all- all the designs are free- so you are free to modify, and add your own. If you come up with very elegant solutions to problems with it- you can even submit them and they may well be integrated back into the design.
Its biggest flaw is the outboard control systems. Maybe these could be made smaller- or exchanged for a bluetooth microcontroller.
